Background

Rakesh Pullabhatla is a Global Finance Executive with an exceptional track record of steering companies from inception through rapid scale — taking organizations from no revenue to over $200M in annual revenue. His career spans startups, mid-market companies, and Fortune 150 corporations across technology, fintech, SaaS, and financial services sectors.

With an MBA from the Booth School of Business at the University of Chicago and dual credentials as a CPA and CFA, Rakesh brings a rare combination of deep technical financial expertise and strategic business acumen. His international experience spans EMEA, CIS, Latin America, and Asia.

Core Expertise

  • Building and leading matrixed, global finance teams from the ground up
  • Creating FP&A, FX & Treasury, and Payment Operations functions
  • M&A due diligence through full integration completion — startups to Fortune 150
  • Upgrading accounting practices to public company standards for IPO readiness
  • ERP implementations and enterprise financial systems transformations
  • Investor reporting, board presentations, and fundraising support
  • Culture change leadership across international, cross-functional teams
  • Rolling forecasts, operating plans, and long-range financial planning
  • KPI development, financial dashboards, and business intelligence

Career Highlights

Rakesh has led M&A transactions ranging from early-stage startup acquisitions to large-scale corporate integrations involving Fortune 150 companies, managing every phase from target identification and due diligence through post-close integration and synergy realization.

He has built finance functions from scratch — establishing FP&A, treasury, and payment operations teams where none existed, putting in place the systems, processes, and talent necessary to scale rapidly. His ERP implementation experience and public-company upgrade work has positioned multiple organizations for successful exits and IPOs.
